Safety

Recommendations

This is an emergency roadside recovery service. 5 simple steps will take no more than 2 minutes.
Your safety is our highest priority. So before you start, make sure that you are in a safe place.
  • If your vehicle's in a dangerous location and drivable, move it somewhere safe now
  • If possible, turn on the side and emergency lights
  • It may be unsafe to stay in your vehicle, so:
    • If possible leave your vehicle safely
    • Wait for assistance as far as possible from the road, in a safe place
  • If you cannot leave vehicle safely, fasten your seat belt
